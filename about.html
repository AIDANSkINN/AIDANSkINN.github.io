About Paint Ball Club!

Discover More About Paint Ball Club

Paint Ball Club was started by Aidan Skinner as a place for friends to gather and have fun with others.

We meet at a specific spot once a month. Locations, dates and times are published here on this website, in the members area one week before our next gathering.

Here are some things we love doing:

  • having fun
  • meeting people and friends
  • playing an exciting game
